About Virtual Pet Adoptions

Adopting a Virtual Friend

We wanted to bring a simulated pet adpoption to life with virtual pets that can do silly things, learn and perform tricks and do all the things a real pet might do, even get sick and may have to see a Vet.

Adopting a pet take it from the Virtual Humane Society and into YOUR device(s) where it will live and interact with you. You can buy it toys with free fake money. You can take it to the park. You may find it being annoying when it's hungry, or when it's just burning extra energy zooming around.
